Meet Keith

I'm a certified Wim Hof Method instructor and life coach based in the Slocan Valley, BC. I teach breathwork, cold exposure, and mindset tools — mostly to young men who are searching for something real in a world full of noise.

I didn't find this work through a textbook. I found it through experience. Three years of self-practice before I attended my first proper workshop and realised I'd been doing it wrong the entire time. That moment changed everything — and it's why I became an instructor.

My classes are small, personal, and practical. You'll leave with tools you can use immediately — not just theory.
