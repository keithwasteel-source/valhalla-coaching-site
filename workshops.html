What Happens in a Workshop

This isn't a lecture. It's an experience.

01

We Talk

Who you are. What brought you here. What you're hoping to get out of it. Every session starts with connection — not a one-size-fits-all script.

02

We Breathe

I'll guide you through the Wim Hof breathing technique — properly. Most people who try it solo get it wrong. You'll feel the difference immediately: tingling, energy, clarity, calm.

03

We Go Cold

Ice baths or cold plunges, depending on location and season. I'll teach you how to breathe during cold exposure — the part most people miss that changes everything.

04

We Debrief

What did you notice? What shifted? How do you take this home and keep practicing? You leave with tools, not just an experience.

Is This For Me?

This is for you if:

• You've never tried cold exposure or breathwork and you're curious
• You've been practicing solo and want proper guidance
• You're dealing with stress, anxiety, or lack of focus and want real tools
• You're a young man looking for direction, challenge, and something that actually works
• You're a parent looking for something meaningful for your son

No experience needed. Seriously. The whole point is to learn. Beginners are welcome — expected, even.
